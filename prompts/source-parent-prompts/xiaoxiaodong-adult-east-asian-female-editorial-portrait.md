## Summary

This is a theme-driven editorial portrait prompt for generating a believable young adult East Asian female character with a strong emotional and narrative frame.

## Reusable Pattern

- Start from `theme / inspiration source`
- Extract:
  - emotional core
  - identity and temperament
  - life state
  - relationship tension
  - sense of era
- Translate those signals into:
  - face and expression
  - clothing
  - hair
  - posture
  - scene details
  - lighting

## Notes

- The prompt is built to avoid repetitive beauty-template generation
- The visual target is `quiet, restrained, intimate editorial portrait photography`
- The last line is the main variable and can be replaced with other themes
